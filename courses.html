About us

The Centre was established in compliance with the Federal Government's initiative through NBTE that all tertiary institutions in Nigeria must establish Entrepreneurship Development Centres (EDC) to solve graduates' unemployment crises by ensuring that they acquire functional entrepreneurial skills, desirable work habits in addition to their formal education.
